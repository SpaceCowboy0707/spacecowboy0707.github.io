介绍 Introduction

I like systems the way I like clothes — simple on the surface, considered underneath.

I'm a data analyst in New York, working across eCommerce, wholesale and dropship channels. Day to day, I trace discrepancies between Power BI and the ERP, clean silver-layer data for a lakehouse build, and automate away the boring parts with Power Automate and AI Builder.

My strength is system-level thinking: dimensional modeling, framing the business question before touching the query, and owning a pipeline end to end. Off the clock I write — Chinese-language essays with a taste for texture and detail — which turns out to be the same skill: noticing what everyone else scrolls past.
