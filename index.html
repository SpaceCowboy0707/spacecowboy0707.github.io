Introduction
I like systems just like the way I live — simple on the surface, considered underneath.

I'm a data analyst based in New York, working across eCommerce, wholesale and retail channels. I build the BI dashboards leadership checks every morning, run deep-dives on pricing, inventory and revenue, and keep the numbers trustworthy — validating data, chasing down discrepancies between Microsoft Fabric and the ERP, and automating the repetitive parts with SQL, Python and Power Automate.

I sit between business and engineering: translating vague questions into requirements, modernizing legacy pipelines into a lakehouse architecture, and turning technical findings into narratives executives can act on. My default mode is end-to-end — from the first stakeholder conversation to the model, the pipeline, and the number on the slide.
